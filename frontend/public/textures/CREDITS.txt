Texture credits
===============

Planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune),
the Sun, Earth's Moon, and Earth cloud map:
  Solar System Scope — https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/
  Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

Titan, Io, Europa, Ganymede, Enceladus:
  Steve Albers, from NASA mosaics (Voyager / Galileo / Cassini / Juno) —
  https://stevealbers.net/albers/sos/sos.html
  Derived from NASA imagery, which is in the public domain.

Callisto:
  Björn Jónsson (bjj.mmedia.is), from NASA/Voyager/Galileo data — public domain.

These are equirectangular surface maps wrapped onto the 3D bodies in
ExploreScene. Bodies without a bundled map render procedurally instead.

To add more bodies (e.g. Europa, Io, Ganymede, Callisto): drop an
equirectangular map named <body>.jpg here and add an entry to
frontend/lib/textures.ts. USGS global mosaics:
  https://astrogeology.usgs.gov/search?pmi-target=europa
